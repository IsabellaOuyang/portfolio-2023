CivicMind | political trivia game app


CivicMind is a mobile game app for 17-23 college students to study more about politics.

PROBLEM

According to our research, it is concluded that younger generation are often indifferent to politics because of their laziness, or their focus on study. Many of them have no idea about politics and how politics relates to them.


PROPOSAL

So, CivicMind is here to educate younger generation about politics through quizzes like trivia games. They can show their intelligence through the battles against their friends and also learn more about politics that are closely related to them, like education, debts, housing, health care, employment, and safety issues.
