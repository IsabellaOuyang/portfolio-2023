WEB DESIGN | women against abusive relationship


In this project, we, teamed up as three, are assigned to design a responsive website for Women Against Abusive Relationship organization (WAAR).
Our goal is to design and create a responsive and welcoming website that displays information about the organization WAAR, seeking to help and prevent women of all ages from abusive relationships through podcasts, books and in-person workshops.

PROBLEM

Women Against Abusive Relationship organization (WAAR) is trying to expand their service and therefore in need of a new website. They want the website to be responsive and calm, leading more women to seek for help when they need it.


PROPOSAL

We choose a color scheme responding to clients' need and try to adjust the color to a warmer tone to create a warm atmosphere. We try to make everything clean and clear for better transmitting the website's message.
