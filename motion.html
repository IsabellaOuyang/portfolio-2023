MOTION | drawing to modelling

This a design-build project based on the motion of a kitchen tool that is assigned. The design of the base is derived from the idea of Yin Yang in Chinese philosophy. Different from the balance of two elements, the design assigns HIERARCHY in the two types of rotation. The sticks try to convey the idea of compression.
