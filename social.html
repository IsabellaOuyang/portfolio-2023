NEW NORM | virtual socializing

This is a screen recording film showing how digital social media has penetrated people's life. Under COVID-19, it has formed a new norm of meeting and communicating with friends, exercising, eating habits, and attending class. When everything starts to become virtual, this film also shows how physical space and virtual space overlaps and interacts.

CHARACTER


The character created for this project is named Gaby. She is a college student who likes food, travelling, enjoying life. She is also addicted to social media.
